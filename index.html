Background

There are two articles about the same event of each case, called "recalled story", and "retold story".
• recalled : 
A memorable or salient story that the author experienced in the past 6 months.
• retold : 
After 2–3 months, we asked the authors to re-tell the events, providing them with the summary of their story as a prompt.

Steps

There are two steps in this lebeling work:
1. Label the spans : 
Select the spans in recalled and retold story, which are related to the event of the summary.
WHAT is events?
1. 
Something exactly happened
EX: I went to Salt Lake City on business with Mr. Wang.
2. 
The sentences which express someone's thoughts or feelings implies that something happened, otherwise, don't select the span.
EX: These cookies taste delicious. -> Imply that the author ate the cookies.
EX: I like Chinese food -> Not imply any event so that don't select these spans.
EX: I’m tired of going to school. -> Not imply any event so that don't select these spans.
3. 
About someone's experience or information.
EX: I skipped fourth grade.
EX: I live in Taiwan.
4. 
Someone's habbit.
EX: I usually look at English language websites every day and go to my local English Corner twice a week.
5. 
It doesn't imply anything but relate to the event of the storys.
EX: (Summary: Me and my girlfriend went to the zoo on a hot day. We saw many animals like giraffes, gorillas, elephants, snakes, tigers, lions etc..)
It was a pretty hot day that day considering it was the middle of July.-> It relates to the event.
7. 
In most cases, the below patterns are illegal.
I feel...
I like...
I think...
I remember...
2. Tag the spans : 
Tag the spans with three types, "Match", "Conflict" and "Add".
Match : 
The spans from both recalled story and retold story describe the same thing, and there are no conflicts between each other.
Notice: No matter which span describe in better detail, we only concern that the spans are describing the same event.
Conflict : 
In contrast to the "Match", The spans from both recalled story and retold story describe the same thing, ,but there are conflicts between each other.
Added : 
If the event only appeared in the retold story, please tag the spans with "Added".
PS:
1 . 
Each group may contain one, two or more span ids.
2 . 
One span may be tag to multiple group, ex: A match with B, but A conflict with C
3 . 
All labeled span of retold story should be tagged
