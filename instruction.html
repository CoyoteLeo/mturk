Background

There are two articles about the same event of each case, called "recalled story", and "retold story".
• recalled : 
A memorable or salient story that the author experienced in the past 6 months.
• retold : 
After 2-3 months, the authors are asked to re-tell the events, providing them with the summary of their story as a prompt.

Annotation Steps

There are two steps in this lebeling work:
1. Label the spans : 
Select the spans of life events in recalled and retold story, which are related to the event of the summary, i.e. Any 5W(When, Who, Where, What, Why) about the event
WHAT is life events
1. 
The events related to personal experiences, e.g.
I went to Salt Lake City on business with Mr. Wang.
2. 
The sentences which express someone's thoughts or feelings implies that something happened, otherwise, don't select the span, e.g.
These cookies taste delicious. -> Imply that the author ate the cookies.
I like Chinesefood -> Not imply any event so that don't select these spans.
I’m tired of going to school. -> Not imply any event so that don't select these spans.
It's a hot day. -> Not imply any event so that don't select these spans.
3. 
About someone's experience or information, e.g.
I skipped fourth grade.
I live in Taiwan.
4. 
Someone's habbit, e.g.
I usually look at English language websites every day and go to my local English Corner twice a week.
5. 
It doesn't imply anything but relate to the event of the storys, e.g.
(Summary: Me and my girlfriend went to the zoo on a hot day. We saw many animals like giraffes, gorillas, elephants, snakes, tigers, lions etc..)
It was a pretty hot day that day considering it was the middle of July.-> It relates to the event.
7. 
In most cases, the below patterns are illegal.
I feel...
I like...
I think...
I remember...
2. Tag the spans : 
Tag the spans with three types, "Match", "Conflict" and "Add".
Match : 
The spans from both recalled story and retold story describe the same thing, and there are no conflicts between each other.
Notice: No matter which span describe in better detail, we only concern that the spans are describing the same event.
Conflict : 
In contrast to the "Match", The spans from both recalled story and retold story describe the same thing, ,but there are conflicts between each other.
Added : 
If the event only appeared in the retold story, please tag the spans with "Added".
PS:
1 . 
Each group may contain one, two or more span ids.
2 . 
One span may be tag to multiple group, ex: A match with B, but A conflict with C
3 . 
All labeled span of retold story should be tagged
